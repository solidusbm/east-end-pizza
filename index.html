Our Story

A family recipe, baked in the Texas Hill Country.

East End Pizza Company is a family owned and operated restaurant located right on the river in Kerrville. Our mission is simple: serve you a slice of paradise so good you'll be dreaming about that stringy, melty cheese for weeks. We're your one-stop shop for great food, great drinks, and great times by the riverside.

East End Pizza Co. is owned by Brick and Jennifer Gibson — also the owners of Brick's River Cafe in Bandera, TX and Attainable Fitness at Riverhill Country Club. They've been serving the Texas Hill Country for the last 19 years, and this brick oven pizzeria is their latest labor of love.

Much love, but no association with West End Pizza in Fredericksburg. 😉
